Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Information We Collect

VT Calendar is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect the following information:

Account Information

  • Virginia Tech email address (@vt.edu)
  • Password (hashed and encrypted)
  • Canvas username and API token
  • Optional Google Calendar and Microsoft account information

Calendar Data

  • Course assignments and due dates from Canvas
  • Calendar events from connected accounts
  • Manual events you create
  • Course information (names, codes, enrollment dates)

Usage Information

  • Login timestamps
  • Session information
  • Feature preferences and settings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide calendar consolidation services
  • Sync your Canvas assignments and due dates
  • Connect your Google Calendar and Microsoft accounts
  • Send you reminder notifications based on your preferences
  • Improve the service and user experience
  • Ensure the security of your account

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All data is stored locally in an SQLite database on the server. Your data is never stored on third-party servers without your explicit consent.

Encryption

  • All passwords are hashed using SHA-256
  • API tokens are encrypted before storage
  • Session tokens are securely generated and managed
  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS

Access Control

  • Only authenticated users can access their own data
  • Session-based authentication prevents unauthorized access
  • Two-factor authentication available for enhanced security

4. Data Sharing

We Do NOT Share Your Data With:

  • Third-party advertisers
  • Data brokers or analytics companies
  • Other users of the service
  • Any external services without your consent

We Only Share Data When:

  • You explicitly connect Canvas, Google, or Microsoft accounts (data flows to those services only)
  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect the rights and safety of users

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data we have collected about you
  • Export: Download your calendar data in JSON format
  • Update: Modify your account settings and preferences
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect: Unlink Canvas, Google, or Microsoft accounts

To exercise these rights, please visit your Settings page or contact us.

6. Third-Party Services

Canvas Integration

  • We use Canvas LMS API to fetch your course assignments
  • Canvas tokens are stored securely and never shared
  • We only access data you explicitly authorize

Google Calendar

  • We use Google Calendar API to sync your events
  • Google OAuth is used for authentication
  • We follow Google's API User Data Policy

Microsoft Integration

  • We use Microsoft Graph API to connect your school account
  • Microsoft OAuth is used for authentication
  • We follow Microsoft's API Terms of Service

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use session cookies to maintain your login status. These cookies:

  • Are essential for the service to function
  • Expire after 24 hours of inactivity
  • Do not track you across other websites
  • Are not shared with third parties

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services.

8. Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained as long as your account is active
  • When you delete your account, all data is permanently removed
  • Session data is automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • Backups may retain data for up to 30 days

9. Children's Privacy

VT Calendar is designed for Virginia Tech students and faculty. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 13 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Posting a notice on the application
  • Sending you an email notification (if applicable)

Your continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

  • Email: ethanl03@vt.edu, jordo@vt.edu, shoumik77@vt.edu
  • Through the Settings page in the application

We are committed to protecting your privacy and will respond to your inquiries promptly.

12. Compliance

VT Calendar complies with:

  • Virginia Tech's data protection policies
  • FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act)
  • GDPR principles for data protection
  • Industry best practices for security
